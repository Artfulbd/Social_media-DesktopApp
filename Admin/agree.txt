Welcome to Artful. A free to use social platform 
dedicated to sharing and reviewing ideas. We at 
Artful are a humble community comprised of you, 
the users, and the moderation panel consisting of
the Super admin who chooses admins to help closely
monitor all posts and member sign ins.
To become a member please enter an NID(National ID)
belonging to you, you’re name, and finally a password
consisting of atleast one character, one number and
one symbol.
If you accept these conditions then we happily welcome 
you to Artful. Please be cooperative and nice to other 
members in order to help make Artful a great platform.
